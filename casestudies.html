--- position: 1 title: case studies ---

With considerable experience in legal recruitment placing senior lawyers both in house and in private practice I have a practical understanding of the legal market place. In recent years, as an Executive Coach I have worked with both Barristers and Solicitors enabling them to manage their careers more effectively. Recent assignments have included:

  • Preparing Barristers for interview for both QC appointments and judicial appointments.
  • Delivering Career Management workshops offering practical advice on networking, use of social media, skills analysis and cvs.
  • Working with senior lawyers wishing to diversify their practice or move in-house. Looking at potential hurdles and offering practical advice on how to overcome these.
  • Running Change management workshops for large law firms going through extensive periods of change.
  • Coaching newly qualified lawyers seeking their first role. Offering advice on dealing with recruitment consultancies, cv writing, interview techniques and how to differentiate themselves in the market place.
