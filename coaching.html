What is Coaching?

Coaching is goal focussed and measurable. It is not counselling. It's purpose is to enable you to be your best; overcoming issues which may be hindering performance by raising your own self awareness and working together to create effective options. There will be 4 stages to the process:

  • Agreeing the goal and identifying the steps that will need to be taken in order to achieve that goal.
  • Looking at what is happening now and what has already been achieved.
  • Working together to identify options and looking at the practicalities of those options.
  • Devising an action plan.

Coaching has many benefits:

  • Clarity around your own goals and how best to achieve them.
  • Independent thinking and ultimately the ability to be your own coach.
  • Motivation to move forwards.
  • Overcoming issues that have been preventing positive action.
  • Greater self-belief and a more positive outlook.
  • Measurable outcomes.
