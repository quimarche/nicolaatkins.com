--- position: 1 title: workshops ---

As an experienced facilitator I enjoy working with groups of people, creating a positive learning environment. I have run workshops for public sector bodies as well as professional firms and universities. Areas covered have included the following:

  • Networking
  • Interview Techniques
  • How to find a job
  • Soft skills
  • Career Planning

Workshops can be run as half or full days and I would recommend that the group is no larger than 10 people.
