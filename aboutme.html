--- position: 1 title: about me ---

For the last 8 years I have been a Career Management Coach working with individuals and organizations to help people manage their careers. Previously I worked in legal recruitment for 6 years, and general recruitment for a further 3 years. I then changed direction to move into outplacement in 2002.

My services

I offer a range of services which can be delivered either on an individual basis or in a workshop format. These include:

  • Full CV Review
  • Advanced interview techniques
  • Assistance with application forms and covering letters
  • Career Direction
  • Job Search Strategy
  • Soft skills development

My qualifications

I am an Accredited Coach; a Member of the Association for Coaching; and on the British Psychological Society's Register of Competence in Psychological Testing for Personality and Ability Tests. In addition I have been accredited by 2 different consultancies to deliver a range of workshops and have consistently received positive feedback from the clients involved.

Full CV Review

Review of existing cv providing constructive feedback, ensuring that the content is relevant to the market place and individual career objectives. Advice on presentation in terms of the 'look', and how to overcome any barriers which might get in the way of you securing an interview. Practical tips on profiles in order to make them relevant and worth reading.

Advanced interview techniques

Introduction to the types of interviews being used in the current market place and what employers are looking for. In-depth look at the types of questions that are asked and, how to answer them, with a particular focus on those questions that you dread being asked. Constructive feedback on body language, tone of voice and content. Advice on useful questions to ask at the end of the interview.

Assistance with application forms and covering letters

Advice on all aspects of application forms including reasons for leaving previous positions, referees and the section which typically asks you to give any additional information supporting your application. In addition will review completed application forms looking at grammar, spelling and relevance of information.

Advice on how to ensure that covering letters are relevant and stand out from the crowd, looking at how to present information that will interest the prospective employer. Tips on how to overcome barriers in covering letters and how to apply for jobs where the job description is very brief.

Career Direction

Reflection on career to date in order to look at what has gone well and what you might like to do differently moving forward. Analysis of skills, strengths and qualities. In-depth look at possible career options and advice on how to research those. Objective and practical advice given as to how easy it might be to pursue chosen career path and how best to proceed.

Job Search Strategy

Identification of key criteria in terms of location, salary and timings. Assistance with research in terms of career options. Advice on how to build effective relationships with recruitment consultants, maximise networking opportunities and using social media. Devising a plan of action for moving forward with regular reviews, looking at what is going well and how the strategy might be improved.

Soft skills development

Looking specifically at communication skills and how to improve those in the working environment. Specific areas covered include managing relationships at work, presentation skills, and management training. Sessions usually involve looking at what is happening currently, what is already working and, how that might be carried into other areas. Identifying practical strategies for moving forward,with regular reviews to look at what has worked well and whether new strategies need to be identified.
